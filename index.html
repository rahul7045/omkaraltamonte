Omkar Alta Monte Malad

Omkar Alta Monte in Malad East is a sprawling 6.5-acre luxury residential project offering unmatched living experiences. Strategically located on the Western Express Highway, it ensures seamless connectivity. The project features a 51-storey tower with stunning views of the Sanjay Gandhi National Park and a gated complex with a robust 7-tier security system. Residents can enjoy over 50 modern amenities, including a 61,000 sq.ft clubhouse, 20,000 sq.ft garden, and a 45,000 sq.ft sports arena. Highlights include lavish swimming pools, a banquet hall, theatres, a library, a gym, and sports courts. The project also includes a sky lounge, children’s play areas, and senior citizen zones. With RERA possession set for December 2024, 3BHK and 4BHK apartments start from ₹2.90 Cr. Omkar Alta Monte also offers unique amenities like an infinity pool, temperature-controlled pool, aqua gym, Jacuzzi, and an elevated jogging track. The property features a Jain and Ganesh temple in the vicinity, adding to the spiritual ambiance. The clubhouse has a pet and children's crèche, an open party lawn, a BBQ area, a yoga room, a spa, and 5 guest rooms for visitors. The sports arena includes badminton, squash, tennis courts, and cricket nets. Managed by Cushman & Wakefield, the project offers a complete lifestyle package, catering to every aspect of luxury and comfort for its residents.
